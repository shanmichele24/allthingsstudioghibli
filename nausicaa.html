Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

Release Year: 1984

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

In a world ravaged by pollution and war, Princess Nausicaä is determined to protect her people and find a way to heal the planet. As the leader of the Valley of the Wind, she’s all about understanding and living in harmony with the Toxic Jungle, a dangerous, polluted forest, instead of just fighting it. Nausicaä’s journey is filled with action, bravery, and a whole lot of heart as she works to bring peace to a world on the edge of collapse
