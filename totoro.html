My Neighbor Totoro

Release Year:1988

Director:Hayao Miyazaki

Two young girls move to a new house in the countryside of Japan to be closer to their mom, who's in the hospital. While getting used to their new life, they discover their home is surrounded by a magical world full of friendly wood spirits and mystical creatures. As they explore, they start to form a bond with the spirits, learning about nature and how important it is to take care of it.
