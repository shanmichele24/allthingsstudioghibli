Princess Mononoke

Release Year:1997

Director:Hayao Miyazaki

Ashitaka, is cursed by a boar demon while trying to protect his village. His journey to find a cure takes him into the middle of a conflict between the humans, who are destroying the forest, and the spirits who protect it. Along the way, he meets a fierce girl named San, who was raised by wolves and is determined to stop the humans no matter what.
