The Boy and the Heron

Release Year:2023

Director:Hayao Miyazaki

Mahito is a young boy grieving the death of his mother. After her passing, he and his father move in with his aunt, who his dad married after Mahito’s mom died. She’s also pregnant, which adds another layer of tension for Mahito. In their new home, Mahito starts encountering a mysterious heron and other magical, supernatural events.Leading him to go on a search for his aunt,Natsuko.
